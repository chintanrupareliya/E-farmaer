1 Drip irrigation yojna The Scheme is being implemented with a uniform subsidy pattern of 50% of the Micro Irrigation System (MIS) cost or Rs 60,000/- per hectare, whichever is less. The tribal (ST) and Schedule Caste (SC) farmers are entitled to an additional subsidy of 25% of the MIS Cost or Rs. 90,000/- per hectare, whichever is less. for more detail click on "visit this" for filling form click on "Form"
2 Solar yojna Gujarat government has launched Suryashakti Kisan Yojana (SKY) 2022 for farmers welfare. Under this scheme, farmers can use solar energy for irrigation purposes and can also sell the surplus power via grid. For installation of solar panels, farmers will only have to pay 5% upfront of the total cost. for more detail click on "visit this" for filling form click on "Form"
3 cold storage yojna What is the cold storage subsidy in Gujarat? Cold storage subsidy in Gujarat will be 25 Lakh per unit with a capacity of 6 MT. And credit linked back-ended subsidy is around 35% of your total project cost in general locations and 50% for hilly and scheduled locations. for more detail click on "visit this" for filling form click on "Form"
4 dron chhantkav yojna Launching the scheme from a village in Gandhinagar today, the Chief Minister said, the use of drones in spraying the nano urea will cut down the agricultural cost substantially which will increase the income of the farmers. The provision of 35 crore rupees has been made for the scheme in the current financial year. for more detail click on "visit this" for filling form click on "Form"
5 PM tractor yojna Under the scheme, eligible farmers are provided 20 to 50 % subsidy for purchase of a new tractor for farming. Scheme is implemented by the state authorities. Subsidy provided by the government is directly transferred to the bank account of the applicant. Applications can be submitted either in online or offline mode for more detail click on "visit this" for filling form click on "Form"
